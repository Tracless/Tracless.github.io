Take a self-test about your crazy catlady potential

Do you feel that one of your friends, colleagues or even yourself is at risk of becoming a crazy catlady or a crazy catguy? Fear no more! Kitten News provides you with an easy to take self-test (suitable for desperate people between the age of 18 and 50) to let you identify and sucessfully prevent the adoption of up to 14 cats into one household.

Down below you find questions about everyday life. To partake in this test, please answer the questions truthfully. Add the points you got by answering the questions and check your score with the diagnosis below. Good luck and may the cats be ever in your favor.

1. What is your personal relationship to cats?

I hate animals of all kinds, I am a superior being in any way. (1 point)

I am more like a dog type of person. (2 points)

I have up to 4 cats myself and I love them. (3 points)

I am legitimaly married to 3 of my cats through a wedding institution from Las Vegas. (4 points)

2. How do you treat your cats?

I put them in a self built colosseum and let them fight for life. (1 point)

I feed them from time to time so I can eat my lunch and dinner in peace. (2 points)

I pet them often and try to make them purr. (3 points)

I try to please my cats 60 hours a week and the only money I make is by putting the cat petting footage on youtube/twitch.tv/periscope/patreon (4 points)

3. How is your personal life going?

I do sports 3 times a week, eating healthy, meeting my friends 3 times a week while also having a beautiful relationship. (1 point)

I try to do sports which happens like once a month for 30 minutes. Going on a friends birthday party every once in a while. Work is sometimes more of a paid torture than living the dream. (2 points)

Work is tough but my cats are happy when I am coming home. Friends and hobbies got extinct. Im living the life I was always scared of. (3 points)

My life had an metamorphose to an apocalyptic mess when I became 20 years old. Sometimes I rob a bank as nobody knows who I am anyway. (4 points)

4. Which type of person are you at a party?

I have fun with alot of people there. I party like there is no tomorrow. (1 point)

I keep it chatty with friends, hitting the dancefloor from time to time. (2 points)

Mostly standing around and standing in a group of people where I have one friend. (3 points)

I am the kind of guy who tries his hardest to catch the beer pong ball and give it to the guys actually playing. (4 points)

5. Which of the statements describes your hobbies the best?

I try to live sporty and have an adventureful life. I already won 2 Olympic Gold Medals. (1 point)

I have a dedicated hobby like playing cards or building stuff. (2 points)

My hobbies are not that exciting, but they give me a break from work, sometimes. (3 points)

My hobbies include stamp collecting, duck photography and speed-knitting. (4 points)

6. Do you have cats already?

Not a single one and probably never will. (1 point)

1 or 2 because they are great for children developing. (2 points)

3-5 mostly because they are lovely pets and improved my life, maybe, somehow. (3 points)

I dont count anything below 7 cats for obvious reasons. (4 points)

7. How would you rate your cat addiction?

I dont even like cats, stupid question. (1 point)

I was always interested in having a cat, but the circumstances were not there. (2 points)

Cats have this magical aura around them which makes my knees weak. (3 points)

Addiction is about losing control of the problem. And I have full control over my 16 cats, no problem here officer. (4 points)

Results

1-7 points You don´t like cats and they don´t like you. You also seem to be happy with your life as it is now. There is no possible risk that you end up with a bunch of cats.

8-14 points Cats seem to be more of a gimmick for you. You are happy to have them in your life, but they aren´t a big part of it. The risk for an addiction is small, although there.

15-21 points Cats are fascinating and your life isn´t running that great. Cats could give you the love you need and take you off the money you don´t have. You probably end up with some cats.

22+ points Cats are the best and only thing that happened in your life. Without them your heart would create a physically working black hole. Your life without cats is meaningless and you know it. Your future self will have a diagnosable psychosis and addiction to fur.
